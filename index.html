My Unforgettable School Story

A memory that stayed in my heart

First Day

When I changed my school in class 4, it was the first time in my life. I felt awkward and nervous because I didn’t know how to talk to new friends.

Meeting Nirdesh

During break time a boy came and sat beside me. He asked random questions. Later I found out his name was Nirdesh Subedi and he was the vice principal’s son.

Friends Warning

Some friends told me not to talk to him because they said he talked badly about others. At that time I didn’t like him much.

Unexpected Friendship

But slowly we started talking and playing together. Soon we became good friends.

The Dream

One night I dreamed someone stole my purse and a man in a black suit caught the thief and returned it to me. When I looked closely, it was Nirdesh.

COVID Separation

Then COVID came and I changed my school again. Even after years, my feelings for him didn’t disappear.

Confession

After almost seven years I told him my feelings through TikTok. He rejected me and even blocked me because I kept messaging him.

Still a Memory

Even today I still remember him and those memories from school.
